Grafity’s Wall

with Ram V, Anand Radhakrishnan, Irma Kniivila and Jason Wordie, published by Unbound Books.

Notes: As Ram is fond of reminding me, hand-lettering Grafity’s Wall was a decision I made in a pub while out drinking with him and Anand, and it ended up a tremendous learning experience. After figuring out a good lettering style and workflow (documented in detail here), I lettered the whole thing on paper over printouts of Anand’s line art and matched it back to the final pages digitally.
