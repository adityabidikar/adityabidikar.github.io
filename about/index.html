About Me

I’m Aditya Bidikar, and I work as a comic-book letterer (current projects include Isola, These Savage Shores, Motor Crush and Bloodborne—you can visit my portfolio here).

I also create fonts that will soon be available for purchase—stay tuned to the ‘Fonts’ section on the left.

I also occasionally write comics and prose, which you can access in the ‘Writing’ section on the left—again, still under construction.

If you’d like to talk to me, I’m on Twitter at @adityab. I sometimes post pictures of work-in-progress and cats I meet on my Instagram. And to comment on the blog, or to talk to me about work, you can always email me at aditya@adityab.net.
